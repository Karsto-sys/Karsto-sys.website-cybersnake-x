Tech Warrior

Become a tech-warrior and defeat the demonic overlords in this intense VR rougelike dungeon crawler.

Wield powerful high-tech weapons and unleash devastating abilities as you fight against demons, fantasy creatures, and other monstrous foes.

Will you become a high-tech warrior and survive against the odds to emerge victorious, or will you fall to the forces of evil?

As you progress through a battle, you will be able to choose from powerful randomized upgrades that can alter your gameplay significantly, allowing you to tailor your character and your weapons to your playstyle.
Are you able to pick the right combinations to slaughter your enemies?

Eliminate foes, acquire resources, improve your weaponry, and repeat the cycle until victory is yours.

Upgrade your weapons with powerful modifications and deploy drones, traps, and bombs to gain an advantage.

Unleash your true potential in combat by strategically utilizing skills like timeslow and berserk.
Do you want to become a glasscannon that unleashes rocket-propelled railgun shots or a tanky mobile minigun that rains down a hail of bullets and lightning strikes on your enemies.

There was an engineer named John who was enslaved by a group of wealthy demons. The demons enjoyed using John for their own entertainment, forcing him to fight for his life in gladiator-style battles using weak and unreliable guns.

John was a skilled engineer, but he had never been trained for combat. He tried his best to survive each battle, using his intelligence and resourcefulness to outsmart his opponents. Despite his efforts, he was constantly injured and on the brink of death.

One day, John realized that the only way he could gain his freedom was to improve the weapons he was given. Using his knowledge of engineering, he began to secretly modify the guns, making them stronger and more reliable.

At first, the demons were pleased with the improved weapons and allowed John to continue using them in the battles. But as time passed, they began to suspect that John was up to something.

GAMEPLAY

Our game, Tech Warrior, will use a classical rogue-like design mixed with a dungeon crawler-based battle system. It consists of randomized rooms and challenging boss fights. After each cleared room, players can choose from 3 powerful yet randomized perks to significantly alter the gameplay.
The core gameplay loop consists of:
▪ Start a run
▪ Acquire resources
▪ Learn & potentially fail
▪ Upgrade weapons & unlock new skills
▪ Try again.
There will be a total of 4 worlds, chained together by an area boss for each world. To finish the game,
the player needs to clear all 4 worlds including their bosses from start to finish.
Inspiration: Hades (gameplay), Doom (Theme / Weaponry), Serious Sam (Gameplay)
We are not going to reinvent the wheel – rather we are going to polish it and make it our own. Our
focus is on making the gameplay as much fun as possible.

THE HOOK

Tech Warrior combines elements of Rogue-like, Bullet hell & Dungeon crawler
▪ Take on the role of a high-tech gladiator – Slay monstrous foes, demons & otherworldly creatures
▪ Combine high-tech guns with demonic melee weapons and magic skills
▪ Choose from powerful randomized perks that alter the gameplay – decide wisely.
▪ Slay enemies, make wise decisions, fail, upgrade and try again
